What is SVends?

SVends is a startup based out of Stanford that is working to make street food vendors in China more trustworthy and accessible. Trust in the integrity of street food is deterring far too many tourists and citizens, we are here to rebuild this trust- to reinvigorate lost cultural dishes.

Unique QR Codes

By labeling every vendor with a unique QR code we can track previously hard to find vendors.

HTML5 Geolocation

By leveraging existing geolocation services, every time a customer scans the street vendors' QR code other customers will be able to see where that street vendor is located.

WeChat

Almost every smartphone user in China has WeChat installed, and because WeChat has a built in QR code scanner all the infrastructure to implement our solution is in place. There is no required download, no update, no friction: just open WeChat and scan away.

Government

The Government has created a faction of the police called the ChengGuan just to regulate vendors. However, because there are millions of vendors that move around the city it is impossible for such a small task force to keep track of all the vendors. We will work hand in hand with the ChengGuan to make sure that our solution meets the interest of the Government as well. With the geolocation of every vendor, found by customers scanning the qr code, the Government now has virtual addresses of all vendors. Making it easier to make sure vendors are selling clean food.

Environment

Allowing customers to rate vendors encourages vendors to keep their carts clean and their food trustworthy. Lets create a cleaner China together.
